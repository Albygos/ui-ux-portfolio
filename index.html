Problems

  • Equipment unreliability

    Users worry about receiving faulty or substandard event gear which could ruin their live events.

  • Hidden charges

    Unexpected setup and logistics fees disclosed at the last stage lead to high cart abandonment.

  • Complex scheduling

    Coordinating delivery, setup, and pickup times across multiple vendors is highly stressful.

  • Last-minute support

    Lack of direct support channels makes resolving order modifications or issues difficult.

Solutions

  • Verified vendor partners

    All items are certified and tested by verified local suppliers prior to delivery.

  • Flat-rate transparent billing

    Clear breakdown of rental, delivery, and setup costs shown on the booking summary.

  • Integrated logistics

    Automated scheduling for on-time delivery, professional setup, and post-event pickup.

  • 24/7 dedicated support

    Real-time support via WhatsApp and phone to handle emergency changes.

User Flow

EventRent User Flow guides organizers seamlessly through catalog browsing, multi-item cart management, scheduling, secure payments, and order tracking.
